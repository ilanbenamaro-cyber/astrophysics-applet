## How This File Works

When the same situation occurs 3+ times across different tasks or workflows,
it becomes a pattern. Patterns either:
1. Get encoded into workflow files (if they're actionable steps)
2. Get encoded into CLAUDE.md constraints (if they're rules)
3. Stay here as awareness for future decisions
