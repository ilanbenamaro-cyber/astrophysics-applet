## Scoring Rubric

```
OBJECTIVE (6 pts):
  All STALE entries addressed (updated or marked with audit comment)  2 pts
  All INCORRECT entries corrected and re-verified                     2 pts
  LAST_REVIEWED date set on all touched files                         1 pt
  No contradictions silently resolved                                 1 pt

SUBJECTIVE (4 pts):
  Review was thorough — didn't just check obvious items               2 pts  [subjective]
  New MISSING entries proactively identified and added                 1 pt   [subjective]
  DEVELOPER-PROFILE accurately reflects current preferences           1 pt   [subjective]

MAX: 10
```
