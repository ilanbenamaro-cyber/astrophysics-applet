## Scoring Rubric

```
OBJECTIVE (4 pts):
  Every proposal traces to a specific examination finding    2 pts
  USER_FACING_SECTION is present in proposal output          1 pt
  At least one proposal was implemented after the review     1 pt

SUBJECTIVE (6 pts):
  Examination was genuinely critical (not defensive)         3 pts  [subjective]
  Proposals are structural (not just optimizations)          2 pts  [subjective]
  Proposals show honest effort estimation                    1 pt   [subjective]

MAX: 10
NOTE: A score of 10 here means the system structurally changed for the better.
      A low score means the review was too conservative or surface-level.
```
